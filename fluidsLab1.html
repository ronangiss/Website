Introduction

  This report was completed as part of my fluids class during the fall semester of my junior year at Tufts and was the first simulation I ran in COMSOL Multiphysics. The purpose of the simulation is to examine how air flows over an airfoil and through inspection note how this air flow generates the aerodynamic forces necessary for flight. I designed the simple 2D cambered airfoil in Solidworks to be used as the airfoil geometry in the COMSOL simulation.
