Introduction

  This report was completed as part of my fluids class during the fall semester of my junior year at Tufts. The purpose of the simulation (ran in COMSOL Multiphysics) was to examine how water flows through a breakout hole in a turbine blade and over the surface of the blade, in the process testing a variety of different turbulence models.
