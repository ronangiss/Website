Introduction

  Another device to be repaired from my grandma. My grandma used this small desk lamp to study in highschool in the early 1950s. Over time, the connections that held together the joints allowing the desk lamp to be moved and repositioned loosened. She asked me if there was any way I could fix it. Given that the joints were riveted, the only way I could see the joints being tight again would be to take out the rivets and replace them with a nice fastener. I broke the wire....
