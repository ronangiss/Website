Introduction

  This project was completed as part of my electronics class during the fall semester of my junior year at Tufts and was the first PCB I designed. The PCB is about 1" x 2" and provides 3.3V, 5V, and 12V connections as well as ground connections for each. The electronic components are mounted using the through-hole method and include the following:


  • 1 Barrel Jack Connector
  • 1 3.3V Voltage Regulator
  • 1 5V Voltage Regulator
  • 3 10 uF Capacitors
  • 1 Screw Terminal (1x2)
  • 4 Pin Headers (1x2)
  • 1 1K Resistor
  • 1 Red LED
